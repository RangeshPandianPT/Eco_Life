# EcoLife - Future Feature Roadmap

This document outlines potential future features and expansions for the **EcoLife** platform to drive user engagement and further promote a sustainable, eco-friendly lifestyle.

## 1. 🦹‍♂️ Eco-Tamagotchi (Virtual Pet/Avatar)
- **Concept:** Provide a virtual avatar or an "Earth" pet that visually reflects your carbon footprint and streak.
- **Details:** If your streak is active and your footprint is low, the pet thrives and the environment looks lush. If you break streaks or have a high footprint, it gets smoggy and sad. A fun, visual way to build emotional investment.

## 2. 🔌 Smart Home & Utilities Integration
- **Concept:** Automatically track home energy and water usage.
- **Details:** Let users securely connect their smart thermostats (like Nest/Ecobee) or smart plugs. The app can analyze real-time electricity and water consumption and suggest targeted missions to lower utility bills.

## 3. ⌚ Wearable & Health App Sync
- **Concept:** Verify eco-friendly transport easily.
- **Details:** Integrate with Apple Health, Google Fit, or Strava. When users take "Walk instead of Drive" missions, the app can automatically verify step counts or bike rides and award points seamlessly.

## 4. 👓 AR (Augmented Reality) Supermarket Scanner
- **Concept:** Next-level grocery shopping.
- **Details:** Instead of scanning single barcodes, users can open the app's AR camera view in the supermarket aisle. The camera overlays an "Eco Score" (A to F) over physical products on the shelf in real-time, helping them instantly spot the greenest items.

## 5. 🌳 Carbon Offset Marketplace
- **Concept:** Give users an actionable way to zero out their emissions guilt.
- **Details:** Once the app calculates a user's monthly CO2e (Carbon footprint), offer a direct portal to fund verified environmental projects (like planting trees or renewable energy startups) to achieve a net-zero lifestyle.

## 6. 🌍 Community Leaderboards & Local Challenges
- **Concept:** Social competition.
- **Details:** Users can join regional leaderboards (e.g., "Greenest User in San Francisco") or team up with friends to complete massive collective goals like "Hit 10,000 meatless meals this month".

## 7. 🥕 AI Gardening & Composting Assistant
- **Concept:** Empower users to grow their own food and manage waste.
- **Details:** A tool where users can log what they want to grow or what scraps they have. The AI provides step-by-step guidance on setting up a compost bin or growing an herb garden based on their location and current season.

## 8. 🔔 Location-Based Smart Nudges (Push Notifications)
- **Concept:** Proactive sustainability.
- **Details:** If the user’s phone detects they are at a grocery store, it sends a quick notification: *"Don't forget your reusable bags!"* If they are leaving home, it reminds them: *"Did you turn off the AC?"*

## 9. 🍲 Sustainable Recipe Sharing Hub
- **Concept:** Expand the AI Meal Planner.
- **Details:** Allow users to publish their favorite low-cost, low-footprint recipes to the app's community. If an uploaded recipe gets highly rated by others, the creator earns huge bonus eco points.

## 10. ♻️ Second-Hand & Upcycling Marketplace
- **Concept:** Promote circular economy.
- **Details:** A localized feed where users can give away clothes, electronics, or surplus food to neighbors, preventing items from ending up in a landfill.

## 11. 🎧 Educational Eco-Podcasts & Mini-Courses
- **Concept:** Built-in micro-learning.
- **Details:** Provide short, 5-minute audio snippets or interactive quizzes on topics like "How to Recycle Plastics Correctly" or "The True Cost of Fast Fashion" to educate users on the go.

## 12. 🚗 EV Route Planner
- **Concept:** Support for electric vehicle owners.
- **Details:** A map integration that not only finds the deepest green route but also syncs with live charging station availability along the way, prioritizing chargers powered by renewable energy.

## 13. 📸 AI Junk-Sorter (Computer Vision for Recycling)
- **Concept:** Take the guesswork out of recycling.
- **Details:** Users point their camera at a piece of trash, and the AI instantly tells them whether it goes into the recycling bin, compost, or landfill based on their local municipality's waste management rules.
